About the Project

The AI-Powered Autism Detection and Emotion Analysis project aims to provide accessible tools for early autism screening and emotion recognition using artificial intelligence. Our mission is to support parents, caregivers, and healthcare professionals in identifying potential signs of autism spectrum disorder and understanding emotional patterns.

Our Mission

We believe that early detection and intervention can significantly improve outcomes for individuals with autism spectrum disorder. Our mission is to harness the power of artificial intelligence to create accessible, user-friendly tools that can help identify potential signs of autism and provide valuable insights into emotional understanding.

While our tools are not diagnostic instruments, they aim to provide an additional layer of information that can support professionals in their assessment and help families take proactive steps toward seeking professional evaluation when needed.

Ethical Considerations

We are committed to developing and deploying AI technologies in an ethical and responsible manner. Our approach includes:

  • Privacy & Data Protection: We implement strict data protection measures and ensure user privacy in all aspects of our platform.
  • Transparency: We are transparent about how our AI models work, their limitations, and the data used to train them.
  • Fairness & Equity: We strive to ensure our tools are fair, unbiased, and accessible to diverse populations.
  • Human-Centered Design: Our tools are designed to augment human expertise, not replace it, particularly in healthcare contexts.
  • Ongoing Evaluation: We continuously evaluate and improve our technologies to ensure they provide accurate and helpful information.

We emphasize that our tools are not intended to diagnose autism spectrum disorder or other conditions. They should be used as supporting resources alongside professional evaluation by qualified healthcare providers.
